CakeFace's Story

CakeFace is not just a bakery, it's a labor of love. The recipes that we use have been perfected over generations, with every ingredient carefully selected and every technique passed down from a grandmother's gentle hand. It's this sense of tradition and warmth that we wanted to share with the world, and so the granddaughter, inspired by her grandmother's legacy, decided to turn her passion into a business. With every pre-made cake and every custom creation, we strive to create not just a delicious treat, but a memory that will last a lifetime. And now, as we expand to catering for parties, we are honored to be a part of even more special moments in people's lives. At CakeFace, we believe that every bite should be savored and every occasion celebrated with sweetness, and we hope to share that belief with you.
